About Me

Adamant about my goals, flexible about my methods

I aim to have an impact which not only imporoves the lives of those around me but also reaches many people across the globe, and I know in order to achieve this, technology will be the key.
This mission, along with my love for math brought me to UC San Diego's Math/Computer Science program in 2019. Once there I learned of a new a fascinating department, the first of which had been founded on the very campus, Cognitive Science. With a specialization in Machine Learning I had the ability to focus more of my courses towards ML AND to learn about data science, technology design, and human computer interaction. This balance allows me to learn the technical aspect of my product, which giving me the insight to design my vision in a form which makes it accessible to all users.
Currently I am a member of the Triton RoboSub's Software/Machine Learning team and am working towards contributing to NASA Jet Propulsion's CoSTAR's entry for the DARPA Subterrainian Challenge.
On my free time I love to cook, read, and practice Jiu Jitsu.
